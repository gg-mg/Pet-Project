The Carnivores of East Africa

The Crocodile (Crocodylus niloticus)

A vicious carnivore that has survived for millions of years.

The populations are stable compared to predators

The Lion (Panthera leo)

The lion is king of the the savannah!

The lion can spend 22hrs sleeping each day

The Leopard (Panthera pardus)

A leopard spends most of it's life on trees and uses stealth to hunt.

It is rare to sight a leopard.

The African Wild Dog (Lycaon pictus)

The African wild dog is the most successful hunter in the wild

African wild dogs hunt in packs with strategy of exhausting the prey
